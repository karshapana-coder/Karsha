From Ancient India to the Kaspa Blockchain

Karshapana was one of the earliest coins in human history, minted in ancient India around 6th century BCE. These silver coins were the currency of trade, culture, and power.

Karshapana 3.0 revives this legacy on the Kaspa blockchain - the fastest, most scalable proof-of-work cryptocurrency. We're bringing the spirit of those ancient marketplaces to the digital streets of today.

This isn't just another meme coin. $KARSHA represents the fusion of historical significance with cutting-edge technology and urban culture. It's money with meaning, powered by the most advanced blockchain.
